About the BJYM

BJYM’s Leadership structure is similar to that of the BJP, with a President, Vice Presidents, General Secretaries, Secretaries, Media Incharges, Social Media Incharges and Executive Members - with the National Executive being the highest decision-making body of the BJYM. At the state level, a similar structure is in place till the mandal level. Established in 1978, Bharatiya Janata Yuva Morcha (BJYM) is the World’s largest Youth based Political Organisation. It is the youth extension of the Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP), the World’s largest Political Party and popularly known as “Yuva Morcha” amongst its followers and sympathizers. BJYM was established as an organization giving voices to the Youth of India. It provides a forum for young people to participate significantly in the socio-political process and develop their natural leadership skills to serve society and the nation.
